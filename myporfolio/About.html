About Me

Hello! I'm Sam Modumo, a passionate self-taught frontend developer based in Koster.

My journey into the world of web development began through self-study and a profound enthusiasm for coding. With dedication and a hunger for learning, I embarked on this journey to explore the intricacies of building immersive and user-friendly web experiences.

Throughout my learning process, I have dived deep into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, constantly refining my skills to create responsive and visually appealing websites. I am also proficient in various frontend libraries and frameworks such as Bootstrap and React.

Driven by the desire to expand my knowledge and create meaningful projects, I am constantly exploring new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in web development.

I am eager to contribute my skills and creativity to projects that challenge me and allow me to grow both personally and professionally.
